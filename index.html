About

I am a student researcher at the School of Statistics and Data Science, Southwestern University of Finance and Economics, Chengdu, China. My work focuses on reliable feature selection and machine learning methods for heterogeneous, unlabeled, and multi-source data.

02

Research interests

Causal Feature Selection

Identifying stable and informative features while reducing confounding-induced spurious associations.

Unsupervised Learning

Learning useful representations and structures from data without relying on expensive labels.

Multi-source Learning

Modeling shared structure and source-specific heterogeneity across multiple data sources.

Time Series Forecasting

Developing robust learning methods for complex temporal and multivariate data.
